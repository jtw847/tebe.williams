About
Where Finance Meets Intelligent Hardware

I'm a long-serving SaaS and AI executive with 25 years of experience across software development and intelligent equipment deployment. I've led teams that deployed over 50,000 kiosks and AI-enabled robots worldwide - products you've encountered in your daily life, from the grocery store and hardware store to the country club.

Most of the time, you don't even realize how intelligent the terminal or equipment is - and that's the point. Game-changing AI products create the consumer experience as it should be, not as it was or with unnecessary bells and whistles.

As CFO, I've guided these companies through two high-value exits - and I know precisely what it takes to build the financial systems, operating models, and strategic plans that transform physical AI concepts into profitable, scalable businesses commanding premium acquisition valuations.
