ChatGPT and Claude? Choose AND Pay for Both

Tebe Williams · March 2026

At some point, everyone downloaded an LLM for the first time. For most people, that moment came with a mix of curiosity and skepticism, and a lot of them are still right there, on the free account, on the same platform, slowly hearing about what other people seem to be doing with these tools that they aren't. There is an incredible difference between those folks and the ones who have moved beyond. I wish I had known then what I know now. Let me share my experience.

Unlearn the assumption that ChatGPT and Claude are basically the same thing. They are not. This is not Google versus Bing versus Yahoo, where you are searching the same internet through a slightly different interface. These models were built differently, trained differently, and they think differently. What one does well, the other sometimes doesn't. What one struggles with, the other handles cleanly. Treating them as substitutes means you are leaving real capability on the table every single day.

Using only one model quietly caps your AI growth. Prompting has become one of the most valuable skills in working with AI, but here is the thing: Prompts are not universal. The way you frame a question, structure a request, or give context to Claude is not identical to how you do it with ChatGPT. If you only ever use one, you think you are getting good at AI. What you are actually getting good at is one AI. The goal should be fluency across the technology, not familiarity with a single platform. The people building real skill are the ones who have to adapt, translate, and think across more than one model.

A free account is not the product. It is a demo. If you want the speed, the capacity, the advanced features, and the ability to actually integrate these tools into how you work at a serious level, you have to pay for it. Both subscriptions together run around forty dollars a month. That is not an expense. For what it unlocks, it is one of the most defensible investments you can make in your own professional capability right now.

Worried about starting fresh with a new LLM? Both ChatGPT and Claude have a built-in solution for this. In ChatGPT, go to Settings, select Data Controls, and click Export Data. You will receive an email with a downloadable file containing your full conversation history. In Claude, go to Settings, select Account, and click Export Data to receive the same thing. Both exports give you a portable file you can save, reference, and use however you need. Your history is not trapped. The switch is easier than you think.

The people who are doing more with AI are not smarter or more technical. They just got curious, stayed curious, and kept going. There is no reason that cannot be anyone.
